# Home page hook video

**Target length:** ~2:30 · **Placement:** homepage hero · **One job:** get the
email signup for the 3 free tips.

---

## V1 — word-for-word

> Read as written. "…" = short beat/pause. (CUT) = safe edit point if you
> flub a take.

Hey — my name is Ethan, and I run Ei Conversion.

And if you're watching this and you run a local business, there's about a 99%
chance you're not ranking in the top three on Google when people look for your
service in your area.

And that's sad… because the top three gets all the traffic. All the calls, all
the visitors, all the leads, all the clients. 75% of people never scroll past
those first three results. Right now your competitors are sitting up there —
and you could outrank them… but you don't.

So let's fix that.

(CUT)

To give you an idea: for the average business in an average city — say,
McAllen — ranking in the top three is worth at least fifty thousand dollars a
year. That's real money being left on the table.

Now, there are basically two ways to get up there.

Way number one: you pay Google. There are actually four spots in that "top
three" — I know that sounds weird, trust me on this — one of them is a paid ad.
Google will happily put you there… for thousands of dollars every month. That's
the good news. The bad news? The moment you stop paying, you're gone.
Instantly. Back to invisible.

Way number two: you get there organically. That means Google itself decided
you're the most relevant result for that search. And the good news is you don't
pay Google a cent. They just rank you. You only have to do a couple of things
right.

(CUT)

That's exactly what I do. I help local businesses rank in the organic top
three — so you get all the perks of being up there, without paying Google
forever… and ever… and ever.

Now — here's what I've done. I put together a video showing you three things
you can do right now to rank higher on Google. No technical know-how needed,
it's easy, and it's the exact same three things I do first when I start working
with a business. It's free. It won't cost you a thing, and it will help you
rank better.

The only thing you have to do: somewhere on this page there's a signup. Drop in
your email and the video comes straight to your inbox.

You'll also get my newsletter — tips that actually work for businesses here in
the Valley. And if you ever stop wanting it, click unsubscribe and I'll never
bother you again. I'm not in the business of bothering people. I'm in the
business of getting people to the top of Google.

Sign up now… and let's get you ranking higher on Google. See you in the next
video.

## V2 — beat sheet

> Hit the **bold lines** close to verbatim — they're doing the persuasion work.
> Everything else, say it your way.

| # | Beat | Job | Must-hit lines |
|---|------|-----|----------------|
| 1 | Introduce | Name + brand, no throat-clearing | "My name is Ethan and I run Ei Conversion." |
| 2 | Call out + pain | Make the viewer feel seen | **"If you run a local business, there's about a 99% chance you're not ranking in the top three on Google."** |
| 3 | Agitate | Stack what they're losing | **"The top three gets all the traffic — all the calls, all the leads, all the clients. 75% of people never scroll past it."** + "your competitors are there… you're not." |
| 4 | Transition | Snap out of the pain | "So let's fix that." |
| 5 | Make it concrete | Dollar-ize the pain | **"For an average business in McAllen, the top three is worth at least $50,000 a year."** |
| 6 | Path 1: ads | Set up the contrast | Four spots, one is paid, "thousands every month" → **"the moment you stop paying, you're gone — instantly."** |
| 7 | Path 2: organic | The better way | **"Google decided you're the most relevant result. You don't pay Google a cent — you just do a couple of things right."** |
| 8 | You = the guide | Position without bragging | "That's exactly what I do — the organic top three, without paying Google forever." |
| 9 | Free value | The lead magnet | **"A free video with three things you can do right now — the same three things I do first with every client. No technical know-how needed."** |
| 10 | CTA | One instruction only | **"Somewhere on this page there's a signup. Drop your email, the video comes straight to your inbox."** |
| 11 | Reduce friction | Kill the "spam" objection | Newsletter mention + **"if you ever stop wanting it, unsubscribe and I'll never bother you again. I'm not in the business of bothering people — I'm in the business of getting people to the top of Google."** |
| 12 | End | Loop them forward | "Sign up now — let's get you ranking. See you in the next video." |

**Improv freedom:** beats 1, 4, 5 (use any Valley city), 8, 12. Keep beats 2,
3, 9, 10, 11 tight — they're the spine.
