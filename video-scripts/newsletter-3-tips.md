# Newsletter / "3 free tips" video

**Target length:** ~7:00 (screen-share heavy) · **Placement:** `/free-tips`
page, linked from the signup email · **One job:** deliver genuinely useful
value so the viewer trusts you, then softly point at working together.

**Demo setup before recording:** open an incognito window, search
**"plumber mcallen tx"**, and check that the map pack (places tab) looks good
on screen — a clear top 3, ideally one strong profile to click into and one
weak/incomplete one to contrast. If plumbers look messy that day, roofers or
electricians in McAllen work as backup. Record the screen at 1080p+ with the
browser at 100% zoom.

`[SCREEN: …]` = what's on screen. `[CAM]` = cut back to your face.
