My name is Ethan, and I run Ei Conversion. I do marketing for local
businesses — it's what I do all day, every day, I know nothing else — and
everything I've learned comes down to just two things you actually need to
know. In this video I'm going to go over those two things. I'm also going to
cover how to figure out whether you and I are a good match… and what happens
when we do end up working together.

(CUT)

The number one thing when it comes to marketing for a local business: you have
to show up at the exact moment someone is looking for your service — when
they're trying to fix their problem. You have to be there. Right… (snap) right
at that moment. If you're not, you lose them. They go to a competitor. It's
over.

Now — showing up at that moment used to be the only thing you needed. But with
Instagram and TikTok, attention spans have gone downhill really, really fast.
Times have changed. So there's a second thing.

Number two: it's not enough to show up once, when they're searching. You have
to keep showing up — basically be everywhere, all the time — from the moment
they've made it clear they need what you do. Because the vast majority of
people don't buy the first time they see you. So you have to keep showing up
over time.

Now let's talk about how you actually do that.

(CUT)

The best way to catch people at the exact moment they're looking… is to be at
the top of Google. And that's exactly what I do for clients. All day, every
day. Within 90 days you'll be ranking in the top three for the search terms
your customers actually type when they're looking for what you do. And I mean
that literally — I guarantee it: top three within 90 days, or I keep working
for free until you're there.

And when that happens — people find you. Calls go up. Website visitors go up.
Leads go up. Clients go up. Everything gets better.

But we're just getting started. Because it's going to be so much better than
that… we have to stay in front of them. It's not enough to show up once. We
have to show up again, and again, and again. Like I said — most people don't
buy the first time they see your stuff.

The way I do that is retargeting, on Google and on Meta. So it doesn't matter
if they're watching YouTube, scrolling Instagram, checking Facebook — every
time they're online, your company shows up again. And that little bell rings in
the back of their head: (snap) "Hey — you wanted to get that fixed, right?
They're right here. Let's get it done."

(CUT)

Once this is set up, something magical happens. Yes — it takes a couple of
months. That's okay. You're going to be in business anyway. Something magical
happens where you become the go-to guy — or the go-to girl — in your market.
Whenever someone looks up your service, they find you… and then they keep
seeing you. And as a result, you get all the mind share. You get all the trust.
You get all the referrals. You're the obvious choice.

People want to do business with the company that's the obvious choice. The safe
choice. The one that's showing up everywhere. And that is going to be you.

So — that's what's possible. Now let me tell you what's required. Because there
are a few requirements.

(CUT)

Number one: I work with businesses that are doing well and want to do even
better. If you're literally at zero and just starting out — we're probably not
a good match. If you're about to go under — also not a good match. But if
you're doing well and you want to scale up, take the next step — we're probably
a good match.

Number two: marketing takes a while to reach its full effect. If you're looking
for a solution that has to work by next weekend, that won't happen. I put in a
huge amount of effort up front, and it compounds over the weeks and months we
work together. That's exactly why my guarantee is 90 days — not 9 days. If you
need it done by Saturday, we're not a good match.

Number three: right now I charge most clients a flat rate of 497 dollars a
month to get them to the top of Google. The retargeting on Google and Meta
depends on your industry and your budget — that part is custom, so we'd work it
out on a call. But the core work — getting you into the top three — is 497 a
month, and for most people, that works.

Fair warning: as the schedule fills up, that price is going up. It's gone up
before, it'll go up again. If you lock in a rate, you keep that rate. But — if
497 a month is prohibitively expensive for you right now… we're also not a good
match. I don't want you spending your last dollar on this. That wouldn't make
sense for either of us.

Number four: I'm not a big fan of gigantic contracts and tons of legalese. If
you need four lawyers and a 48-page contract — we're probably not a good match.
If we both have the right goal in mind, we'll get along. And if at any point
you're unhappy, or you feel like "hey, we've done enough" — just tell me. We'll
call it off. No hard feelings. We go our separate ways.

(CUT)

So — the next step is to book a call. And I want to ask you: please, please,
pretty please… the reason I put the requirements in this video is so you know
where you stand. If you heard them and thought "that's me" — then I think we're
a great match, and you should book the call.

There's no sales floor here. No closers, no setters. It's going to be me on
that call. And my days are mostly filled with client work — actually moving the
needle — so if you're not there yet, that's okay. Wait until you are.

But if you are: there's a form right below this video. Fill it out, we'll find
a time that works, and we'll talk — and see if we're a good match.

Looking forward to it.
